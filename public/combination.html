Dry & Oily Skin Care Guide

Expert advice, remedies, and the best products to manage dry and oily skin types.

Dry Skin Care

Dry skin lacks moisture and can feel tight, flaky, or irritated. Follow these guidelines for relief.

Remedies for Dry Skin

  • Use a hydrating, fragrance-free cleanser.
  • Apply rich moisturizers that lock in hydration.
  • Use a humidifier to maintain moisture in dry environments.
  • Avoid hot showers, which can strip your skin's natural oils.

Precautions for Dry Skin

  • Choose skincare products without alcohol or fragrances.
  • Wear sunscreen daily to protect from UV damage.
  • Avoid scrubbing or over-exfoliating, as this can cause further irritation.

Oily Skin Care

Excess sebum production can cause oily skin. Here's how to control shine and maintain balance.

Remedies for Oily Skin

  • Use an oil-free, foaming cleanser to remove excess oil.
  • Apply a lightweight moisturizer to keep the skin hydrated.
  • Incorporate a clay mask to absorb excess oil.
  • Exfoliate regularly with products containing salicylic acid.

Precautions for Oily Skin

  • Avoid oil-based makeup products that can clog pores.
  • Do not over-wash your face, as this can stimulate more oil production.
  • Always wear a mattifying sunscreen to prevent greasy residue.
