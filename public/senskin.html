Sensitive Skin Care Guide

Gentle care for delicate, easily irritated skin.

What is Sensitive Skin?

Sensitive skin is prone to redness, itching, burning, or dryness. It reacts easily to skincare products, weather, and environmental factors.

Common Triggers

  • Fragrances and dyes in skincare or detergents
  • Harsh weather (wind, sun, cold)
  • Over-cleansing or exfoliating
  • Alcohol-based or acidic products
  • Stress or lack of sleep

Skincare Tips

  1. Use fragrance-free, hypoallergenic products.
  2. Patch test new products before full use.
  3. Cleanse with mild, non-foaming cleansers.
  4. Moisturize regularly with soothing ingredients (like aloe or oat extract).
  5. Avoid scrubbing or over-exfoliating.
