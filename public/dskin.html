Dry Skin Care Guide

Keep your skin hydrated and healthy with the right care.

What is Dry Skin?

Dry skin, also known as xerosis, occurs when the skin lacks moisture. It can feel rough, tight, flaky, and may even crack in severe cases.

Causes of Dry Skin

  • Cold or dry weather
  • Hot showers or baths
  • Harsh soaps and detergents
  • Medical conditions (eczema, psoriasis)
  • Aging

Skincare Tips

  1. Use gentle, fragrance-free cleansers.
  2. Moisturize immediately after bathing.
  3. Use thick creams or ointments instead of lotions.
  4. Avoid hot showers; use lukewarm water instead.
  5. Use a humidifier to add moisture to the air.
