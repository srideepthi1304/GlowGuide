Oily Skin Care Guide

Understand and manage your oily skin the right way.

What is Oily Skin?

Oily skin is caused by excess sebum production from the sebaceous glands. It can lead to clogged pores, acne, and a shiny complexion.

Causes of Oily Skin

  • Genetics
  • Hormonal changes
  • Stress
  • Hot and humid climate
  • Overwashing the face

Skincare Tips

  1. Use a gentle foaming cleanser twice daily.
  2. Apply oil-free moisturizers.
  3. Use non-comedogenic products.
  4. Exfoliate 1-2 times a week.
  5. Use blotting papers to control shine during the day.
