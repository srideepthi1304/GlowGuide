Daily Skincare Routine

This is a sample routine. Adjust as needed for your specific skin type and concerns.

Morning Routine

  • Cleanse: Use a gentle cleanser suitable for your skin type (oily, dry, combination, sensitive). Apply to damp skin, massage gently, and rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.
  • Treat (Optional): Apply a serum targeting your specific concerns (e.g., vitamin C for brightening, hyaluronic acid for hydration).
  • Moisturize: Apply a moisturizer to hydrate your skin. Choose a moisturizer appropriate for your skin type.
  • Sunscreen: Apply a broad-spectrum sunscreen with an SPF of 30 or higher. This is crucial for protecting your skin from sun damage.

Evening Routine

  • Cleanse: Remove makeup and cleanse your skin using a gentle cleanser. Double cleansing (oil-based cleanser followed by a water-based cleanser) may be beneficial if you wear makeup.
  • Treat (Optional): Apply a nighttime serum or treatment (e.g., retinoids for acne or anti-aging).
  • Moisturize: Apply a night cream or moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated overnight.

Note: This is a general guideline. Consult a dermatologist for personalized advice based on your specific skin needs and concerns. Always patch test new products before applying to your entire face.
