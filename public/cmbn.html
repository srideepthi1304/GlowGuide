Combination Skin Care Guide

Balance is key when caring for combination skin.

What is Combination Skin?

Combination skin features both oily and dry areas—commonly, the T-zone (forehead, nose, chin) is oily, while the cheeks are dry or normal.

Common Traits

  • Shiny T-zone but dry cheeks
  • Visible pores on the nose and forehead
  • Occasional breakouts in oily areas
  • Dryness or flakiness in other areas

Skincare Tips

  1. Use a gentle cleanser that doesn’t over-dry.
  2. Apply lightweight moisturizers that hydrate without clogging pores.
  3. Use oil-absorbing products in the T-zone.
  4. Spot treat oily and dry areas differently.
  5. Use non-comedogenic, alcohol-free skincare products.
