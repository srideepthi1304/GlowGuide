KNOW YOUR SKIN BETTER

OILY SKIN

Oily skin has a porous, humid and bright appearance. It is caused by excessive fat production by sebaceous glands, and usually determined by genetic and/or hormonal causes. Use a cleanser that's oil-frees

Know More

DRY SKIN

In many cases, dry skin is caused by external factors such as the weather, low air humidity and immersion in hot water, and it is usually temporary. However, for some people it may occur more often and even be a lifelong condition.

Know More

SENSITIVE SKIN

Sensitive skin is easily irritated by factors like weather, skincare products, or even stress. It often feels itchy, tight, or gets red quickly. People with sensitive skin should use gentle, fragrance-free products and always do a patch test before trying something new.

Know More

COMBINATION

Based on its location, it presents characteristics of both dry and oily skin since the distribution of sebaceous and sweat glands is not homogeneous. The area with more oil is usually the T- zone (forehead, nose, and chin).

TAKE CARE!!

OILY SKIN

Needs: Lightweight, non-comedogenic (won’t clog pores), gel-based or matte finish sunscreens.
Recommended SPF: SPF 30–50
Look for: Oil-free, "dry touch", or "mattifying" labels.

DRY SKIN

Needs: Moisturizing sunscreen with hydrating ingredients like hyaluronic acid, glycerin, or ceramides.
Recommended SPF: SPF 30–50
Look for: Cream-based, hydrating, or nourishing sunscreens.

SENSITIVE SKIN

Needs:Gentle, fragrance-free, and mineral/physical sunscreens (with zinc oxide or titanium dioxide).
Recommended SPF: SPF 30–50
Look for:Hypoallergenic, dermatologically tested.

COMBINATION

Needs: Balanced sunscreen — not too oily, not too drying.
Recommended SPF: SPF 30–50
Look for: Lightweight lotions or gel-cream textures.
